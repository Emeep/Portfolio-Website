About

Ever since I was young, I fascinated over efficiency and automation. From that fascination, it aspired me to become a software developer, one who aims to make everyone's life easier.

Hard Skills


I am self-taught in Python as my main language. I use python for most of my task automation projects and computer vision projects using libraries such as OpenCV and NumPy. I also use Python for AI projects and are familiar with libraries such as Keras and Tensorflow.

I use Linux as my daily driver. I have high level of proficiency in shell scripting and configuration. I am also experienced in different linux distributions such as Ubuntu, Fedora, and Arch Linux.

I have experience in web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript/TypeScript. I am familiar with frameworks such as Bootstrap, TailwindCSS, and React.

In addition to web development, I also have experience in app development for Android and IOS using React Native and frameworks like Expo Metro.
